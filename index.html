About Us

Our Story, Vision & Values

Our History

Founded in 2005 in Nairobi, Kenya, Corban Construction began as a small road construction firm and has grown into a regional leader in civil engineering projects.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's premier infrastructure development partner, delivering innovative and sustainable solutions that connect communities and drive progress.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services through technical excellence, quality workmanship, and commitment to client satisfaction while fostering local talent.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Innovation, Safety, and Sustainability guide every project we undertake and every relationship we build.

Our Journey

2005

Company Founded

Established in Nairobi with initial focus on rural road projects in Kenya.

2010

Regional Expansion

Successfully completed first international project in Uganda, marking our regional expansion.

2015

Diversification

Expanded services to include bridge construction and water systems development.

2020

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management systems.

2023

Major Milestone

Completed the landmark Mombasa Southern Bypass project, our largest to date.

At Corban Construction, we recognize that our work has lasting impacts on communities and the environment. We are committed to implementing sustainable practices across all our operations.

Environmental Protection
